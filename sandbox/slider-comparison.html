Slider anatomy comparison

Bu sayfa, slider pattern icin iki markup yaklasimini ayni icerik ve ayni gorsellerle karsilastirir. Birinci ornek track/viewport katmani kullanir; ikinci ornek dogrudan wb-slider > wb-slide yapisini kullanir.

Track'li surum Horizontal transform, swipe ve sonsuz kaydirma gibi davranislar icin daha acik bir hareket katmani verir.
Sade surum CMS renderer icin daha dogal: slider dogrudan slide cocuklarini tasir. Fade/crossfade davranisi icin yeterlidir.
Media testi Iki slider da gercek img kullanir. Tam kaplama object-fit: cover ile cozulur.

Track'li anatomy

Studio

Layered work, quiet rhythm

Media and content are independent layers. The image covers the full slide while text keeps its own readable width.

Workshop

A flexible content slot

The content area can hold a heading, rich text, buttons, cards, or CMS child blocks.

Gallery

Good for motion-heavy sliders

Track gives JavaScript one explicit element to translate when slides move horizontally.

<div class="wb-slider" data-wb-slider>
  <div class="wb-slider-viewport">
    <div class="wb-slider-track">
      <article class="wb-slide">
        <img class="wb-slide-media" src="..." alt="">
        <div class="wb-slide-content">...</div>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

Sade anatomy

Studio

Layered work, quiet rhythm

Same image, same content, fewer structural elements. The slide itself owns media and content.

Workshop

A flexible content slot

This maps closely to CMS parent blocks: Slider contains Slide blocks; Slide contains child content blocks.

Gallery

Best default for CMS output

For fade or crossfade, direct slides are enough and the public markup stays smaller.

<div class="wb-slider" data-wb-slider>
  <article class="wb-slide">
    <img class="wb-slide-media" src="..." alt="">
    <div class="wb-slide-content">...</div>
  </article>
</div>

Img cover vs background cover

Tam genislik ve yukseklik icin background-image sart degil. Sol kutu gercek img ve object-fit: cover; sag kutu background-size: cover. Gorsel kaplama davranisi pratikte ayni.
