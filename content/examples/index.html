Complete pages to inspect

Examples are meant to be opened and dissected.

Open these pages, inspect the markup, and lift patterns from working interfaces instead of isolated snippets.

Inspect first

  • Read the HTML

    The value is in the structure and class composition, not in a hidden abstraction.

  • Start with shells

    Lift the page frame first, then replace the inner content with your own.

  • Trim hard

    Keep only the classes and sections your real page actually needs.
